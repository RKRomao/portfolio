NeoLAAC

NeoLAAC (Linha de Apoio ao Caloiro) is a digital platform designed by and for students at the Universidade da Beira Interior (UBI). Its primary goal is to act as a "digital mentor," simplifying the transition for incoming freshmen by centralizing academic, logistical, and social resources to reduce anxiety and disorientation.
