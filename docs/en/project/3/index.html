CyberCrow

16/06/2026
Cybersecurity Ethical Hacking Digital Privacy OSINT Penetration Testing Red Team Blue Team Information Security RGPD

The CyberCrow Project is a comprehensive cybersecurity and ethical hacking platform in the Portuguese-speaking community dedicated to democratizing digital security knowledge through open-source tools, privacy education, and offensive/defensive security training.

Technologies:

Python, Linux Shell Scripting, Git

Features:

Ethical Hacking Education: Practical, hands-on tutorials, specialized labs, and resources tailored for both beginners and advanced penetration testers. Privacy & Anonymity Advocacy: Deep-dive guides on secure browsing, cryptography, VPN implementation, and navigating alternative networks like Tor. Open-Source Tooling: In-house development and distribution of custom scripts and automation tools designed for security auditing and vulnerability scanning. OSINT Frameworks: Dedicated training and tools focusing on public data collection, digital footprint mapping, and investigative tech. Collaborative Ecosystem: A vibrant Portuguese-language community providing peer-to-peer mentorship, CTF coordination, and real-time threat intelligence sharing.

Detailed Description:

The CyberCrow Project acts as a multifaceted ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between complex cybersecurity concepts and community-driven learning. Established to cater to IT professionals, aspiring ethical hackers, and regular digital citizens alike, the project emphasizes both Red Team (offensive security/penetration testing) and Blue Team (defensive security/incident response) methodologies. Beyond formal learning, CyberCrow operates as a highly active community hub where members collaborate on Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges, analyze real-world vulnerabilities, and share open-source scripts. A core tenet of the project is its unyielding focus on digital privacy, anonymity, and Open Source Intelligence (OSINT), equipping individuals with the practical tools and knowledge required to safeguard personal data against modern cyber threats.
